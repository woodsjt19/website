Study Scheduling

Study scheduling can be useful because it allows studying to become more organised instead of more like a scramble to learn all of the required information for whatever is being studied as fast as possible.

Study scheduling is the process of planning when to study ahead of time in a weekly schedule. Studying in this way can prevent cramming as if you stick to it and don’t make it at the last minute, you‘ll have learnt pretty much everything you need to before the test or deadline.

A good study schedule can be made with basically anything that allows the creation of a table, e.g. Excel, google sheets, or it could just be made with a ruler and pencil. Just make a table with each day as a column and each part/hour of the day as a row, and fill in when you want to study each day. Even an hour of study every day still adds up very quickly, and you’ll have a major advantage in tests and assignments.
